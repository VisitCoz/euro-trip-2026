🗺️ Where you stay & what to see

London 🇬🇧
5 nights · 8–13 Jul
Land 07:05 on the 8th — first day is pure rest (jet-lag + Leo). Then take it slow: Westminster & Big Ben, Tower Bridge, a ride on the London Eye, easy park days (Hyde Park, St James's) and a market (Borough or Covent Garden). Miguel's parents are here too; his dad flies home on the 10th.
Helsingborg 🇸🇪
5 nights · 13–18 Jul
Your family's home in southern Sweden — the cozy heart of the trip. Relax with family and the Skåne nature: the Kullaberg coast and Mölle, Söderåsen forest & lakes, Sofiero's gardens, and the beaches & harbour. Copenhagen is ~1 hour away across the bridge if you want a city day.
Paris 🇫🇷
4 nights · 18–22 Jul
The grand finale. Eiffel Tower & Trocadéro, a walk along the Seine, the Louvre & Tuileries gardens, Montmartre, and an optional day at Versailles. Slow mornings in the parks with Leo. Fly home on the 22nd.
